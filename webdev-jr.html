Introduction

Want to build your own website? It’s no secret that one of the best skills you can acquire today is Web Development. Even just the basics can open new doors of learning and opportunity for your career.

Instead of watching Netflix or hanging out with your buddies, just dedicating 60 minutes two times a week to learning web development consistently could easily allow you to gain the knowledge you need.

If you’re wondering whether it’s possible to learn web development basics which includes HTML, CSS, Bootstrap, JavaScript, and more from home for free, the answer is yes!

Coding Blocks Junior, an initiative of Coding Blocks is announcing the launch of Web Development Junior course for School Students. The course is absolutely free of cost and can be opted by just subscribing to the Coding Blocks Junior YouTube channel.

What's more? You will also be provided with a Certificate of Completion upon successful completion of the course.
