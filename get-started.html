How to get started ?

Learning to code has many advantages, especially for School students. Here are a few to list

  • Better Analytical and logical ability
  • Better Abstract thinking
  • Higher scores in Mathematics
  • Higher confidence
  • Better Career prospects
Coding Blocks Jr will teach Coding free to School students. Courses on Block / Visual programming, Python, Web development, Robotics and Data science are available and live classes in batches would be conducted continuosly.

To subscribe to the courses, learn and get certified, you need to do the following
  • Go to CB Jr Youtube channel, click to visit our Youtube channel
  • Subscribe to the channel
  • Select your course on our Youtube page and start learning
